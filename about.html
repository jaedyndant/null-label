About NULL

We started NULL because we were tired of music being treated as content.

Founded in Berlin in 2018, NULL is an independent electronic music label built on a simple conviction: the best music doesn't optimise for anything. It doesn't chase streams, court playlists, or flatten itself for algorithmic consumption.

We work with artists who treat sound as material — sculptors, not performers. Every release exists because it had to, not because a release schedule demanded it. We press vinyl because the format demands commitment. We release digitally because access matters. We never do both just to cover bases.

Timeline
2018

NULL founded in a Neukölln basement. First release NUL-001 pressed on a 300-run with hand-stamped sleeves. Sold out at Hard Wax in three weeks.

2019

Roster grows to six artists. First NULL NIGHT at Tresor draws 400. Label office moves to a shared space in Kreuzberg.

2020

Pandemic pivots: launch of the NULL digital series. Eight releases in twelve months. Bandcamp becomes primary revenue stream.

2021

Return to physical. NUL-015 "Concrete Memory" becomes a club staple. First international bookings for roster artists in Amsterdam, London, Tbilisi.

2022

In-house mastering studio built. Partnership with Berlin Community Radio for monthly show. Roster at ten artists.

2023

NUL-020 milestone. First album release (Kessler "Void Architecture"). NULL NIGHT becomes quarterly residency at Tresor.

2024

Twenty-four releases deep. The label has never pitched a playlist, never hired a PR agency, and never released a record we didn't believe in completely.
